============================
PAGE 1 - TEMPLATE CREATION
============================

Template Creation
- Start w/ Frequency (# weeks + # days/wk)
- 3 categories => (1) Maintain  (2) Grow  (3) Specialize (or emphasize)
- All "emphasis" muscles get trained early in the wk & early in the day(s)
  they are trained

Example:
  Maintain:   Quads, Calves, Hamstrings, core, back  (Tier 3 priority)
  Grow:       Chest, triceps                          (Tier 2 priority)
  Emphasize:  Biceps, Shoulders                       (Tier 1 priority)
  Frequency:  4 days/wk

---
Wk 1

Day 1               Day 2               Day 3               Day 4
Bicep Exercise 1    Shoulders Ex 1      Biceps Ex 1         Shoulders Ex 1
Bicep Ex 2          Shoulders Ex 2      Biceps Ex 2         Shoulders Ex 2
Chest 1             Quads Ex 1          Core Ex 2           Chest Ex 1
Tricep Ex 1         Calves Ex 1         Back Ex 1   	    Triceps Ex 1
                    Hamstrings Ex 1
---

Put "Emphasis" muscles 1) early in the session, 2) high days/wk
frequency, and 3) Give them more volume/wk over the course of a meso. 

Then all "Grow" muscles are still trained frequently, but not as emphasized and
fewer set accumulation through the meso. 

Finally, "Maintain" is just muscles that you want to train once/wk to make sure they don't atrophy,
but won't expect them to grow much. (Use Maintenance Volume (MV)/Min Effective Volume (MEV)/Minimum Adaptive Volume (MAV)/Max Recoverable Volume (MRV))

Potentially put a guide for prioritizing on the template creation slide
- Only 2-3 MAX muscles prioritized in a meso.


============================
PAGE 2 - SORENESS TRACKER
============================

At the start of the 2nd time working a muscle group, and every time after, ask for soreness from last session and adjust that day's session if necessary.

Example:

Day 1           Day 2           Day 3
Biceps          n/a             Biceps

@ Start of my first bicep exercise on Day 3 ask me "How sore did your
Biceps get after last session?"

  [Cat. 1: Never Got Sore]  [Cat. 2: Healed a while ago]  [Cat. 3: Healed just on time]  [Cat. 4: I'm still sore]

  -> If "Maintain"   I want cat 1 or 2 throughout whole meso
  -> If "Grow"       I want cat 2 or 3 during end of meso
  -> If "Emphasize"  I want cat 2 or 3 during more (most) of the meso
     If Cat 4 => Reduce/Eliminate scheduled set (user choice)

Adjust next week's volume up/down according to the soreness data collected
here and the 3 Tier categories and the MEV/MAV/MRV landmarks

Start everything @ MV or MEV in wk 1
Bring "Grow" + "Emphasize" up to MAV in wks 2-3
Bring "Emphasize" up to MRV by end of meso + keep "Grow" @ MAV to end
This combined w/ soreness/pump/volume data, give the volume
  for the next weeks. Bias soreness/pump data
